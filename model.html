Model used

The model used is _very_ simplistic for now. Refering to an infographic of "Our World in Data", we are taking the average of the CO2 emissions for beef, lamb (both very CO2 expensive ruminants) and pork and poultry (less CO2 expensive).

(60 + 24 + 7 + 6) / 4 => 24.25 kg CO2 per kg meat (crude average ...)
